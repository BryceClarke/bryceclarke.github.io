I am a postdoctoral researcher at Inria Saclay in Palaiseau, France, where I am working with Gabriel Scherer and Noam Zeilberger in the PARTOUT team.

Before that, I was a PhD student of Michael Johnson at the Centre of Australian Category Theory (CoACT) and Macquarie University in Sydney, Australia.

I am broadly interested in category theory and its applications, and so far my research has focused on lenses, (op)fibrations, cofunctors, and double categories.
