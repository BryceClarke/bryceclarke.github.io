Bryce Clarke

email: bryce.clarke1@hdr.mq.edu.au

I am a PhD student, supervised by Emeritus Professor Michael Johnson and Professor Dominic Verity.

I am a member of the Centre of Australian Category Theory (CoACT) and the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia.

I am interested in category theory, and my current research focuses on lenses, opfibrations, and cofunctors.
